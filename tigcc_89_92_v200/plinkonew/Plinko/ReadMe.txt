=============
INTRODUCTION
=============

The classic Price is Right game, brought to you graphically, complete with question rounds 
to compete for Plinko chips, and a very nice Plinko board. Its greatness lies in its simplicity. 
-- By Fisch2


==============
REQUIREMENTS
==============

1) Send the APLINKO.83p file to your calculator
2) Run the APLINKO program (listed under the PRGM key)

If you need help sending the program to your calculator, please consult the ticalc.org tutorial 
first before emailing me questions -- located here:  http://www.ticalc.org/basics/linking/

Compatibility Notes:

* Supported calculators include:  TI-83, TI-83+, TI-83+ Silver Edition, and the TI-84 Plus/SE
* This program is written in TI-Basic and is compatible with MirageOS


=============
INSTRUCTIONS
=============

After several question rounds to compete for Plinko chips, drop your chips down the board by 
pressing ENTER, attempting to get the 10G's!  Compete with friends for high scores, and if 
you can, try and find the secret cheat!
