Get ready to play a full-featured Sumo Wrestling game, complete with everything from pre-match smack talk, to earthquake-inducing tosses, to creating your own customized wrestlers!  Features include Two-Player link matches, intense button-smashing, yet strategic gameplay, built-in sumo editor for creating your own wrestlers, a wide variety of moves and creative finishes, well structured C source code included, Runs Fast and Smooth on HW1 and HW2 68k Calcs, and the graphics are awesome.  All sixteen built-in wrestlers are official, top-ranked Japanese Sumo Wrestlers.  The game will run on all 68k calcs, including the Ti-89 Titanium.
